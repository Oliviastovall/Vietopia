About & Contact

We are a small restaurant specializing in Vietnamese Cuisine in Harrisonburg VA, located right off exit 245 on I-81. Vietopia is honored to have served the Harrisonburg community for 20+ years. Though we have lived through many changes with our neighbors, our recipe is one that has provided a place of constant comfort for many folks from our local residents and college friends in Harrisonburg to travelers on I-81 and across the country.
We offer Dine in, pick up, and delivery through third party services like Doordash. Our seating capacity is 52 and the largest party we can take is 20. We seat on a first come first serve basis, but reservations are welcome.
